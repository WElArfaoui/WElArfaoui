### About

I build **reproducible pipelines for medical image analysis** at the intersection of
clinical research and machine learning. My work spans CT-based **radiomics**, deep-learning
**segmentation** (nnU-Net, TotalSegmentator), **DICOM/PACS** infrastructure, and
GDPR-compliant data de-identification for multicenter respiratory cohorts
(post-COVID imaging, lung cancer screening, sleep medicine).

- 🔬 Bioinformatician
- 🧠 Deep learning for **imaging biomarkers** and **survival / competing-risks** modeling
- 🌍 Español / arabe (native) · English (B2) · Italiano (B1)
